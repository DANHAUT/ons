What is the next step for your students now?

The nominated students will be contacted via email and receive the EDHEC online application procedure and all other relevant information regarding their studies in Lille or Nice.
They will be asked to provide us (via our online application) with the following documents:

- 1 recent ID picture (JPEG)

- Copy of passport or NID (PDF)

- CV (in French or English)

- Copy of the most recent official transcript (PDF)

- Copy of the Bachelor degree if applicable (only for students nominated for a MSc)

- EDHEC learning agreement. Please make sure your students are fully aware of your academic requirements to validate their exchange before entering the online application.

Please, feel free to contact us if you need any further information,
