What is the next step for your students now?

Nominated students will be contacted via email early May with all the necessary information relating to the online application and deadlines, as well as all other information relating to their studies.
Please note that all students, and particularly Non-EU students, should complete the application and upload their documents (ID photo, passport and official transcript) as soon as possible so that we may start processing admission letters necessary for Visa applications.


STEP 1. ONLINE APPLICATION AND DOCUMENTS: DEADLINE May 17th 2022

Students will be asked to provide us (via our online application) with the following documents:

- 1 recent headshot (JPEG)

- Copy of passport or NID (PDF)

- Copy of the most recent official transcript of grades (PDF)

- *EDHEC International BBA course choice form: (See step 2). Please note that course registration will take place at a later stage and the EDHEC International BBA course choice form is not yet available. Students should, however, commence their applications and upload the other documents by May 17th.

*STEP 2. COURSE REGISTRATION AND COURSE CHOICE FORM: DEADLINE June 15th 2022

As soon as the course registration is open, nominated students will receive an email the relevant information on the registration procedure and deadlines.

Important note: there is no add & drop period at EDHEC. The course selection registered online represents the students' final course choice. Students should not select more than 33 ECTS for their semester (30 ECTS being the recommended course load for 1 semester).
Please kindly ensure that your students are fully aware of your academic requirements to validate their exchange before choosing course on our online application.

We look forward to welcoming your students to EDHEC International BBA, and please do not hesitate to contact us should you need any further information.
